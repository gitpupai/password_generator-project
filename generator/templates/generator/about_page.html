This is a simple website to generate password for random works for daily life. You can generate customized difficulty level passwords.

1. Length: You can choose your password length (6-12 characters)

2. Upper Characters: You can choose a password containing at least one uppercase character

3. Alphanumeric: You can choose a password containing at least one number

4. AlphaUpperNumeric: You can choose a password containing at least one upper and at least one number
